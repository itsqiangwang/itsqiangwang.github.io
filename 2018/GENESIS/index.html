Qiang Wang is a Los Angeles-based designer from Fujian, China. he makes identities, posters, print publications, websites, and animations with a focus on typography. you can get in touch or download his résumé.
genesis
An immersive three-screen projection with a narrative based on Taiwanese essayist Lung Ying-Tai's 1994 book Children, Take Your Time (孩子你慢慢来). the author compares a mother's conception and companionship of her child to genesis. i attempt to narrate this profound sentiment with type in motion.
