Genesis
info
Animation, projection mapping, exhibition
May 2018
An immersive three-screen projection with a narrative based on Taiwanese essayist Lung Ying-Tai's 1994 book Children, Take Your Time (孩子你慢慢来). The author compares a mother's conception and companionship of her child to genesis. Genesis attempts to narrate this profound sentiment with type in motion.
