A LOT _________
A LOT _________ is a publication that explores the social and public potential of parking lots – a salient yet underutilized feature of the modern American landscape. all photographs were taken on a parking lot that used to be a popular drive-in theater, now repurposed as a weekly flea market. the publication investigates the physicality of this space and documents the human as well as the material culture within this microcosm. A LOT _________ encourages its viewers/readers to think about the potentials of a parking lot.
