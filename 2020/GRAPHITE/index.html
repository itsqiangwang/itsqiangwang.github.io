GRAPHITE 11
Publication for GRAPHITE, a Los Angeles-based arts organization dedicated to producing critical and creative projects. Its 11th issue Fruit focuses on anti-imperialist and intersectional narratives. the publication presents each contribution from the 34 artists as individual pieces of fruit, with a full-bleed snapshot of their work as the skin and the detailed content of their work as the core.
