UCLA DMA Summer Institute 2019

Identity, Typography, Motion

The Department of Design Media Arts (DMA) at UCLA offers a two-week summer program introducing high school students to design practices for print, web, video, and game. Based on the theme Next Nature, the curriculum for this yearâ€™s program challenges the students to think about what our collective Next Nature could be. The identity centers around a lively typeface featuring cogwheels and organic forms, eluding to an optimistic though speculative future characterized by an interdependent relationship and a balance between technology and nature. The cogwheel as an industrial object representing technology is in motion with the organic lines and curvatures representing nature. These graphic elements of the letterform communicate a sense of tension but also harmony. The typeface is animated and applied to section titles associated with the program, as seen on the website here.
