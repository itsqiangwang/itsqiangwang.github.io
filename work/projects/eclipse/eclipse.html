A poster project created for my Drawing + Color class during Winter 2017.

The goal of this project was to "analyze a concept used by any of the artists discussed in class and create a graphic content for a poster inspired by their color or drawing technique".

The artist of my choosing is Olafur Eliasson. Taking ideas from the artist's installations ( x x ) and the subject matter of his works, I created series of posters focusing on the use of monochromatic color with a central theme about the universe, specifically eclipse. A total of eight variations were designed and one was chosen to be printed as the final product.
