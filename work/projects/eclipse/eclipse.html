A Poster
Series
Graphic design
Typography

Photoshop
Illustrator

17 in. x 22 in.
Digital + Print

January 2017

A poster project created for the 'Drawing + Color' class during Winter 2017.

The goal of this assignment was to:
"Analyze a concept used by any of the artists discussed in class and create a graphic content for a poster inspired by their color or drawing technique. Integrate concepts that we have worked in during class."

The artist of my choosing is Olafur Eliasson. Taking ideas from the artist's installations ( x x ) and the subject matter of his works, I created series of posters focusing on the use of monochromatic color with a central theme about eclipse.
