PALM

Book, Typography

A Palm can reveal a lot. Observing one's palm allows a unique perspective into their life. Reading one's own palm is as much about trying to foresee the future as it is about examining the present. It is an overlooked art and a way of inspecting the self.

PALM is a book documentating my own palm lines, their interpretations, and my own reflections over the span of two months from October 1st to December 7th, 2018. PALM examines four areas: Relationships, Health, Personality, and Work.
