Illitera

Typography, Poster, Motion

Nicolas and Tobias presenting their thesis project at a TED talk

Poster designed in invitation by Nicolas Bernklau and Tobias Müller for their Bachelor's thesis in Media Design. The poster features a quote by Hassan Jando, a non-literate participant who was asked to design a typeface. The quote reads Egal wie alt der Mensch ist er kann immer was lernen, which translates into English as No matter how old a person is, they can always learn something.
